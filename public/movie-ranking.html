The Best Movies According to Andra

My top3 movies of all-time

Dangerous Beauty

Short description: A Venetian courtesan becomes a hero to her city, but later becomes the target of an inquisition by the Church for witchcraft.

Impresions:Beautifull historic love story. Loved the piece of History that is depicted, the characters and the images.

Matrix

Short description:When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld, he discovers the shocking truth--the life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.

Impresions:Extraordinary SF. Interesting new ideeas. Gorgeous images.

Shakespeare in Love

Short description:The world's greatest-ever playwright, William Shakespeare, is young, out of ideas, and short of cash, but meets his ideal woman and is inspired to write one of his most famous plays.

Impresions:Beautifull love story. Heart-wrenching ending.
